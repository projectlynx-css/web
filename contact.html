FAQ

Common Questions

Who can join Project Lynx? +

Any Al-Kawthar University student interested in computer science, technology, or digital innovation is welcome — from complete beginners to experienced developers.

Is there a membership fee? +

Membership is free for all Al-Kawthar University students. Some premium events may have a nominal registration fee to cover logistics.

How do I participate in CTF competitions? +

Join Project Lynx and watch our announcements for the next CTF-451 season. No prior experience is needed — we run beginner-friendly workshops before every competition.

Can I propose a workshop or event? +

Absolutely! Use the contact form above and select "Event Collaboration" or "Speaker Inquiry." We review all proposals and respond within 48 hours.
