About Us

Who Are We?

At the intersection of innovation, curiosity, and collaboration, Project Lynx stands as the official Computer Science Society of Al-Kawthar University.

We are a dynamic community of students, thinkers, and technology enthusiasts committed to pushing the boundaries of computing and digital innovation.

From coding challenges and workshops to AI research, software development, cybersecurity events, and hackathons, Project Lynx transforms ideas into real-world solutions.

Whether you are a beginner exploring programming or an experienced developer seeking meaningful collaboration, there is a place for you here.
